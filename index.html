Core Engines

Three Engines. One Governance Intelligence Layer.

SentinelIQ combines three proprietary modules into a single governance intelligence infrastructure the only platform that unifies internal policy accountability and external supplier reliability into a composite Governance Health Score for SMEs.

Engine 01

Policy Accountability Engine

Digitally distribute, version-control and track acknowledgements of every internal policy. Real-time coverage percentages, automated escalations, and an immutable audit log give leadership complete visibility.

Supports operational, HR, safety, data protection and compliance policy types. Configurable escalation thresholds. PDF/CSV export of acknowledgement evidence packs.
Engine 02

Supplier Reliability Engine

Log categorised supplier issues, track delivery timeliness, and watch our weighted scoring model compute reliability scores continuously. Risk alerts fire automatically when patterns exceed configured limits.

Issue categories include delivery delay, quality failure, communication failure, and contractual breach. Recency-weighted scoring ensures recent events carry more weight than historical outliers.
Engine 03

Governance Intelligence Dashboard

A composite Governance Health Score (0–100) unifies signals from both engines into a three-band risk view Healthy (green), At Risk (amber), Critical (red) updated in real time for leadership action.

Pre-aggregated dashboard APIs for sub-second rendering. Exportable governance snapshot reports. Configurable weightings per tenant for sector-specific tuning.

The Platform

Every Layer of SentinelIQ's Governance Intelligence

SentinelIQ is a multi-tenant SaaS platform built on a modular monolith architecture React frontend, NestJS backend, PostgreSQL with row-level tenant isolation, and AWS cloud infrastructure designed to serve SMEs from 20 to 200 employees without any compliance expertise.

Module 01

Policy Accountability Engine

CRUD policy management with version history, role-based distribution, timestamped digital acknowledgements, and real-time coverage calculation. Immutable audit log captures every policy event.

Cron-based escalation scheduler for non-acknowledgement thresholds. Exportable PDF/CSV acknowledgement evidence packs. Supports operational, HR, safety, data protection, and compliance policy types. Configurable escalation windows per policy criticality level.
Module 02

Supplier Reliability Engine

Supplier profiles, categorised issue logging (delivery delay, quality failure, communication failure, contractual breach), delivery timeliness monitoring, and a weighted recency-aware reliability scoring engine.

Risk alert engine triggers notifications when supplier scores breach configured thresholds. Issue frequency, recency, and severity all feed the weighting model. Portfolio-average scores surface supplier risk trends before they cause disruption. Informed by real FMEA and RCA methodology.
Module 03

Governance Health Dashboard

Composite Governance Health Score (0–100) aggregated from both engines via a normalised, weighted signal processor. Three-band risk view: Healthy (green), At Risk (amber), Critical (red).

Pre-aggregated dashboard data APIs for sub-second rendering. Exportable governance snapshots in PDF/CSV. Daily score records for trend analysis. Configurable weights stored per-tenant for sector-specific calibration. Colour-coded risk indicators on all mobile screen sizes.
Module 04

Security & Audit Architecture

AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.2+ in transit, JWT authentication with refresh token rotation, and server-side RBAC enforcement. Immutable event logs for all data mutations critical for dispute evidence.

GDPR compliance with UK/EU data residency, right-to-erasure workflows, and privacy-by-design data minimisation. API rate limiting against abuse. Pre-launch penetration testing scheduled. ICO registration completed.
Module 05

Risk Signal & Notification Layer

Automated risk signal processing transforms raw governance events into directional alerts. Three supplier delays in 60 days triggers an alert not just a data point. One critical policy below 60% coverage is flagged as exposure, not an admin note.

Notification centre for overdue acknowledgements, supplier risk escalations, and governance score deterioration. AWS SES transactional email for escalation reminders. Webhook system for third-party automation integrations (Year 2). Batch escalation reminders dispatched with a single click.
Module 06

Cloud Infrastructure & Integrations

AWS primary cloud with RDS PostgreSQL, S3 document storage, CloudWatch monitoring, and Secrets Manager. Docker + AWS ECS single-region MVP deployment with multi-region roadmap from Year 2.

GitHub Actions CI/CD with separate staging and production environments from day one. Year 2 integrations: BambooHR, HiBob (HR sync), Xero/QuickBooks (supplier payment cross-reference), SAML 2.0/OAuth 2.0 SSO. Redis caching for session management and dashboard data aggregation.
Proprietary IP

The Governance Health Scoring Algorithm

SentinelIQ's composite scoring engine is a proprietary multi-variable weighting model. Inputs policy acknowledgement coverage, open supplier issue count, portfolio-average supplier reliability scores, response time to escalations, and recency weighting are normalised to a 0–100 scale, weighted by configurable importance coefficients, and combined into a single Governance Health Score.

FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need a compliance team to use SentinelIQ?

+
No. SentinelIQ is specifically designed for SME operations managers and directors without compliance backgrounds. The dashboard-first layout, colour-coded risk indicators, and minimal-click workflows mean you get immediate governance intelligence without any specialist training. Our design principle: an untrained SME manager should understand their governance posture within 10 minutes of opening the platform.

How is SentinelIQ different from HR software like BambooHR or PeopleHR?

+
HR platforms treat policies as documents they distribute them but don't analyse governance risk patterns or integrate supplier oversight data. SentinelIQ treats governance as an intelligence operation. We convert policy acknowledgement rates and supplier issue patterns into directional risk signals that tell leadership what to do next not just what happened.

How quickly can we generate an audit-ready governance report?

+
Under five minutes. SentinelIQ maintains an immutable, timestamped audit history of all governance activities policy versions, acknowledgement history, supplier issue records, escalation responses and bundles them into a formatted, exportable evidence pack on demand. No manual retrieval, no formatting work.

Is our governance data secure and GDPR-compliant?

+
Yes. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+). Data is hosted in UK/EU AWS regions. We implement privacy-by-design data minimisation, right-to-erasure workflows, and ICO-registered data handling practices. Penetration testing is planned pre-launch and annually thereafter.
